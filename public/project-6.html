Weather App

It's a weather app that allows users to check forecasts for cities using the OpenWeather services API, providing details on temperature, airspeed, humidity, climate, etc.

Live Link

Project Overview

The Weather App project is designed to provide users with detailed and accurate weather forecasts for various cities by utilizing the OpenWeather services API. This application offers comprehensive weather information, including temperature, airspeed, humidity, climate conditions, and more. Users can easily search for and select their city of interest to receive up-to-date weather data. The app's user-friendly interface ensures that all the essential weather details are presented clearly and concisely, making it a valuable tool for anyone needing reliable weather updates. Whether planning daily activities or preparing for travel, this Weather App helps users stay informed about current and upcoming weather conditions.
